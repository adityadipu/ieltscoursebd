Interactive Word Count Judge

Rule: "No more than TWO words and/or A NUMBER".

Instructions: Articles (a, an, the) COUNT as words!


Common Scoring Rules

  • Spelling Matters: Incorrect spelling = 0 Marks.
  • Grammar Matters: Sentences must be grammatically correct (e.g., singular vs plural).
  • Capitalization: You can write in ALL CAPS (e.g., GARDEN) or standard (Garden). Both are accepted.
  • Hyphens: "Part-time" counts as 1 word.

Key Strategies

  • Signpost Words: Listen for "However", "But", "Actually". The answer usually changes after these!
  • Plurals: Be careful! "Student" vs "Students". If you miss the 's', it's wrong.
  • Prediction: In the preparation time, read the question and GUESS the type (Is it a number? A place? A name?).
  • Moving On: If you miss an answer, panic is your enemy. Move instantly to the next question.
